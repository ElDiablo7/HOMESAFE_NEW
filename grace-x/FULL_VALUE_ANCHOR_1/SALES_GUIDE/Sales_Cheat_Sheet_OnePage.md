# SALES CHEAT SHEET: AUTOMATION
## Key Stats & Scripts for Sales Calls

### ⚡ THE "SPEED WINS" STATS
*   **5 Minutes:** The "Golden Window" to reply. After this, lead quality drops 21x.
*   **50% of Sales:** Go to the business that responds **FIRST**.
*   **42% of Enquiries:** Happen **AFTER HOURS** (5 PM - 9 AM).
*   **85% of Missed Calls:** Will **NOT** call back. They call the next competitor.

### 💰 THE "COST OF MISSING OUT"
*   **Plumber/Trades:** Miss 3 calls/week @ £250/job = **£39,000/year LOST**.
*   **Salon/Beauty:** Miss 10 DMs/week @ £60/job = **£31,200/year LOST**.
*   *Pitch:* "My system pays for itself if it saves just ONE job a month."

### 🎯 TOP INDUSTRIES TO TARGET
1.  **Home Services:** Plumbers, Electricians, HVAC (High Urgency)
2.  **Beauty/Salons:** Hair, Lashes, Aesthetics (High Volume, Visual)
3.  **Events:** DJs, Photographers, Venues (High Ticket)
4.  **Auto:** Detailing, Mechanics, Tinting (Hands-on, Can't Type)
5.  **Fitness:** PTs, Gyms, Yoga (Impulse Buys)

### 🗣️ POWER PHRASES
*   "Your voicemail is a graveyard. People don't leave messages; they call the next guy."
*   "Be the business that's 'Always Open'—make money while you sleep."
*   "Why pay for ads if you're going to miss the calls they generate?"
*   "Stop playing 'phone tag'—let the customer book instantly."

### 🤖 TOP 5 QUESTIONS TO AUTOMATE
1.  Price/Quotes ("How much?")
2.  Availability ("You free Friday?")
3.  Location ("Where are you?")
4.  Hours ("You open Sunday?")
5.  Services ("Do you do [X]?")
